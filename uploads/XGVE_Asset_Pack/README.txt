XGVE ASSET PACK
================

Contents
--------
source/
  Original attached raster artwork.

primary/
  Horizontal lockup on black, transparent, dark-on-transparent, white-background,
  and 4096-pixel-wide exports.

icon/
  Standalone XGVE face/monogram icon in primary, dark and monochrome variants.

wordmark/
  XGVE wordmark with and without the tagline, supplied on transparent backgrounds.

web/
  Transparent and black-background icon sizes from 16px to 512px, lossless WebP,
  and a multi-resolution favicon.ico.

Notes
-----
- The supplied artwork was a raster PNG, so these are high-resolution raster exports,
  not true editable vector paths.
- Primary red/white transparent assets are designed for dark backgrounds.
- Files ending in "_dark" are intended for light backgrounds.
- Transparent PNGs retain antialiased edges and remove the original black matte.
